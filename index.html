Mercy Masukwa is the founding and Principal Consultant at Reed & Maarly, a cross-border legal and policy consultancy. With over a decade of experience in legal academia, private practice, and international advisory, she specializes in intellectual property, international trade, and private international law.

Fluent in English, Chichewa, and French, she is known for her cross-cultural fluency and her commitment to empowering creatives, entrepreneurs, and institutions across Africa with the legal tools to thrive in global markets.
